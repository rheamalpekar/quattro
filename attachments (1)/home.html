Choose a category that fits your adoption plan.


  • Domestic Infant Adoption

    • Domestic infant adoptions occur when a birth parent legally consents to an adoptive placement and an adoptive family is prepared to care for the child as their legal parents.

  • Foster Care Adoption

    • Foster care adoption is the adoption of a child in foster care whose biological parents’ rights have been terminated by a court. The child may be adopted by either his or her foster parents or another adoptive family

  • International Adoption

    • Inter-country adoption, also known as transnational adoption is a mode of adoption in which an individual or a couple, outside India becomes the legal parent(s) of a child.
